# Faded Palette

A 2.5D tile-based dungeon crawler about advancing to the next floor while defeating 
the "color corrupted" along the way in a presumably colorless world.

## The Scenario
Hearing strange rumors of colored candy, the protagonist wanders 
into a colorless forest in search of color facing the "color corrupted" while trying to reach the candy.

## The Goal
As you move through a terrain that generates randomly as you move, you will have to find the
"clear tile" which looks like candy to get to the next floor.  Use the compass to help track 
down the clear tile while avoiding or fighting monsters.  There is only one floor as of now.

## Controls
Use WASD for basic movement - Tapping it moves once, and after holding down keys for about 1.5s, can move
continously.  The player can also move diagonally.

Use Q and E for camera rotation.

## Combat System
There are two cards on the screen, each of them can be a random one - Attack, Defend, or Heal
Use mouse to click on cards for combat.
Attack can only be used near enemies and cards will go on a 1-turn cooldown
HP is located on top left of the screen
Compass is located on top right of the screen
Cards are located on bottom right of the screen

## Combat Visuals
The player sprite will changed to a damaged sprite when 40% hp or below
When attacking or attacked, tile with the corresponding damage dealt to will highlight red
When healing, tile will highlight green

## Music/Sound
Use headphones for best experience.
